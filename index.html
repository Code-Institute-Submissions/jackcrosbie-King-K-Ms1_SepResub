About the Band

King K are a long running band based out of Kinsale, Co. Cork.
Although the line up has changed the goal of King K has always stayed the same.
To provide good entertaining music while playing a slightly alternative selection of songs.
For more on this check out the song list further down the page.
Below is some testimonials from previous gigs, weddings or events.

Testimonials

Meet The Players

Ronan Daly

Ronan has been involved in music for as long as he can remember. He has been the drummer for King K longer then he wishes to remember.

A multi-instrumentalist who when not playing the drums or in his recording studio can be found cutting crystal in Kinsale Crystal.

Mark Crosbie

Mark has been playing piano since he was 4 years of age. He began his time in King K as the original drummer but after a bit of travelling, and time off, he came back as the keys player.

He comes from a musical family with a jazz background. When not playing music he can be found either on or under water.

Ben Dillon

Ben Dillon has a cool dog. His dog is named Dexter. He's the best. Ben sometimes plays guitar and sings but he's not as cool as his dog.

I wish his dog was the lead singer of King K. He's a really really cool dog. Like the best dog. Just sayin'

Clem Buckley

Clem is the lead guitarist in King K. He has been playing music for most of his life and been involved in numerous different bands and performances.

He has a degree in Music from the Cork school of music and most recently wrote a guitar teaching book. He is a successful music teacher in his own right.

Mike O'Connell

Mike is the longest serving member of King K as he was the original bass player. Apart from playing with King K Mikey also plays with the internationally recognised Mick Flannery.

Mike has played on many recordings, done radio appearances and international tours. He also has a great beard.
